What's the game about?

Blackbeards Treasure is inspired by Angry Birds and takes place in the pirates era. The environment is destructible and there are powerup circles that can upgrade your cannonballs.

Can you succesfully defeat the pirates and claim Blackbeards treasure?

Exam Project

For this exam I have to design and create a game in 1 week (40 hours) while managing a structured backlog/planning, daily standups & sprints, playtesting, clean code and assets.

I started with creating a GDD (game design document) which contains everything about the game, including sprints, sprint reviews, playtesting and a retroperspective.

After the GDD, I created a TDD (technical design document) where I explain how I'm going to code everything.

During the exam I held daily standups with my project supervisor and let others playtest everytime I finished a task, which gave me valuable feedback on what could be improved.

I had 2 sprints in total (5 days for both combined) and used Trello to plan everything.

What did I learn?

Creating my own assets

For this project I drew my own assets, which I've never done before for other projects.
I used paint.net to draw the assets, which all turned out great. The reason I created the assets myself is because I got bored of looking at the default squares and circles.
At first I thought it was a bad priority to take, but I still managed to finish everything I planned in my sprints.

Working with a structured backlog & planning

Because I only had 40 hours to work on this project, I had to plan all my tasks into sprints and figure out how to code everything ahead of time in the TDD.

I handled the entire project very professionally with how I planned and documented everything. I worked hard during the 40 hours of development and I’m really proud of what I’ve managed to get done.

Other Information

Blackbeards Treasure took me 5 days to make in Unity and you can play it on Windows.
