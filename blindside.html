Blindside

Blindside is a top down action game where you strategically overcome menacing threats using your swap ability to get to their weak spots.

Multidisciplinary Project

For the final project of year 1 at BUas I collaborated in a multidisciplinary team with fellow students from Programming, Design & Production and Visual Arts. As a Programmer I worked on gameplay level ingredients, Unreal Engine Tooling and technical features that support both the gameplay and workflow.

Research Topic

My primary focus this project was on my research topic, which was to implement Fog as a Post Process effect. I ended up creating a distance-based dynamic fog as a post process material inside of UE5. The material has many exposed parameters, which allows designers and artists to easily adjust the fog's appearance to their liking.

Documenting

Throughout the project I documented the complete research process, including the motivation behind the topic, looking into how the State of the Art does it, technical implementation details, proof of concept development, and my reflection on the final result.
